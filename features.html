Farmer and Milk Collection

Our easy interface for milk collection is customized for milk collection, SNF collection and both. It carries several features like custom price per farmer, protsan, transportation, TS and may more. We make recording of daily milk easy via our mobile application which we provide single login free with our DairyCo package.

Manufacturing Process

Manufacturing of simple product like dahi, ghee, milk , paneer along with complex items like cheese, churpi, butter can easily tracked with our manufacturing feature of dairy co. which easily manages stock and wastage report.

Employee Record System

Daily payroll of staff can be easily maintained with our employee feature. Which automatically calculates staff payroll each month accordance of recruit date.

Purchase and Sales

Purchase and sales feature works in various ways in dairy co. like purchase of any product from suppliers can be recorded in suppliers’ section of our product likewise farmer sales, employee sales, distributes sales, counter sales come along various features along site.

Accounting and Reporting

Accounting and report feature provides various customized reports like farmer related reports, expenses report, sales and stock report along with individual ledger of farmers, distributors, suppliers etc. Customized reporting feature of DairyCo. Enables possibility to request for more report as per customer’s demand.
